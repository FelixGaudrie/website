Finally, bring robotic application in the Real World!

Reachy is designed to evolve among humans. He is safe and pleasant to interact with and he can make himself useful by taking or giving objects

All-in-one Robotics and AI humanoid platform

Reachy elegantly brings object manipulation, expressivity, machine learning, human-robot interaction in one easy-to-use platform you can install anywhere you need it.

The most expressive robot?

Communicate with your user and create empathy with Reachy thanks to his unique head design and motion.

His free-moving head is animated by in-house developed Orbita© technology and antennas to convey emotions to its audience (happy, sad, excited...). He mimics human expressions and body language in his own unique way: we guarantee Reachy will make you smile!

Modular advanced robotic arm for objects manipulations

Make Reachy useful thanks to his ability to manipulate objects and act on his environments.

With 7 DOF, Reachy’s arms feature similar dimensions, proportions and movement as in an adult human arm. One arm can lift objects that weight up to 500 grams and manipulate them with pretty good dexterity.

His tooltip gripper can be modified to fit at best the target application.

A machine that learns

With internal hardware accelaration for machine learning (TPU) and compatibility with state-of-art AI frameworks (e.g. Tensorflow), Reachy includes all you need to make him learn new things!

Explore smoothly machine learning and reinforcement learning:

  • with his 2 cameras, run objects, face and pose detections at highspeed
  • with his microphone array and powerful speaker, create vocal interactions with people

Easy to setup and autonomous without internet connection

Only 4 screws and a standard 110/220V power plug, it's all what you need to install and run Reachy.

You can easily fix reachy where you need with his simple mechanical interface.

His back give you access to common IO such as USB3.0, HMDI & Ethernet. You even have an extension hole if you want to extend Reachy with your own device.

Two on/off buttons let you the perfect control over Reachy's computer & motors power supply.

Modular and evolutive

Customize your own Reachy with only the modules you need for your usage. And make Reachy evolve over time by adding new modules.
