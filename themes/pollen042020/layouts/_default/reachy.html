All-in-one Robotics and AI humanoid platform

Reachy elegantly brings object manipulation, expressivity, machine learning, human-robot interaction in one easy-to-use platform that you can install anywhere you need.

The most expressive robot?

Communicate with your user and create empathy with Reachy thanks to his unique head design and motion features.

His free-moving head is animated by in-house developed Orbita© technology and antennas to convey emotions to its audience (happy, sad, excited...). He mimics human expressions and body language in his own unique way: we guarantee Reachy will make you smile!

Modular advanced robotic arm for objects manipulation

Make Reachy useful thanks to his ability to manipulate objects and act on his environments.

With 7 DOFs Reachy’s arms feature similar dimensions, proportions and movements as in an adult human arm. One arm can lift objects up to 500 grams and manipulate them with pretty good dexterity.

His tooltip gripper can be modified to fit at best the targeted application.

A machine that learns

With internal hardware accelaration for machine learning (TPU) and compatibility with state-of-art AI frameworks (e.g. Tensorflow), Reachy posesses all you need to make him learn new things!

Explore smoothly machine learning and reinforcement learning:

  • with his 2 cameras, run objects, face and pose detections at highspeed
  • with his microphone array and powerful speaker, create vocal interactions with people

Easy to set up and autonomous without internet connection

Only 4 screws and a standard 110/220V power plug, it's all what you need to install and run Reachy.

You can easily attach Reachy where you need with his simple mechanical interface.

His back provides access to common connectivity such as USB3.0, HMDI, Ethernet, Bluetooth & Wifi. You even have an extension hole if you want to extend Reachy with your own device.

Two on/off buttons give you the perfect control over Reachy's computer & motors power supply.

Fully customizable and evolutive

Customize your own Reachy with the onlyp modules you need for your specific application. Make your Reachy evolve over time by adding new modules and hardware updates.

A complete and efficient development platform
