An expressive head for interaction 

The head is animated by Orbita, a unique technology developed by Pollen Robotics' R&D team. This ball joint actuator allows unpreceded dynamic and multi-directional movement.  With animated antennas, Reachy can convey many emotions to his audience (happy, sad, excited...).

Humanoid arm made for object manipulation.

With 7 degrees of movement, Reachy’s arm features similar dimensions, proportions and movements to an adult human arm.

Modularity to make the robot you will love

Reachy is powered by Luos and comes in modular parts you can combine according to your needs.

Open source platform to develop your interactive application

Reachy can be easily programmed in Python and offers ready-to-use operational environments (playing noughts and crosses, serving coffee, playing musical instruments, handing out specific objects,...).   He is also fully customizable with open hardware, software and data!
